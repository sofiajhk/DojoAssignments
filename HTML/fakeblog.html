Haruki Murakami


Haruki Murakami is a Japanese fiction and nonfiction author born on January 12, 1949. His books are not only bestsellers in Japan but they are immensely popular internationally as well. Millions have been sold in 50 languages leading to numerous awards. His work has not only been translated into different languages but has also been made into notable films.
